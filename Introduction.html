    Food & Beverage of Soluxe Hotel Guangzhou

    Fabulous Social Space to Savor Your Favorite.

    To continue the fine tradition of 'Eating in Guangzhou', Soluxe Hotel Guangzhou is honored to offer a range of gourmet for a ion of settings. 7 luxurious-designed restaurants/bars lead the new trends of dining fashion in town. Nivika Cafe offers buffet and all-day a-la-carte dining of international favorites in a light, airy and open atmosphere. Yue Yue Chinese restaurant serves excellent Cantonese and Huaiyang cuisine. In the surroundings of bridges over flowing streams, we create a poetic atmosphere for dining. 19 elegant private rooms offer the greatest level of privacy and excellent venue for business and private dinner. Flag lounge is the best place for a lazy afternoon enjoying inviting desserts in the surround of flowing streams. For pre- or post-dinner drinks, Vinsi Bar is the obvious choice. The perfect match of wine and cigar is the perfect escape from daily routine.

    Conference of Soluxe Hotel Guangzhou

    Soluxe Hotel Guangzhou boasts ideal venues and meticulous service. From small meetings, to product launches or corporate-wide celebrations, the hotel ensures all your conferences and corporate events are memorably excellent. Totaling over 3,600 square metres of meeting and banquet space to suit every occasion, the hotel houses the Ji Yang Ballroom with a stately space of 1,100 square metres and a maximum capacity of 1,100 people，one 625 square metres function hall and 13 meeting rooms in a ion of sizes. All venues are professionally equipped with wireless Internet access and state-of-the-art audiovisual equipment. The leading city 360-degree optical projection system in the Ji Yang ballroom provides a marvelous meeting experience. The hotel's experienced and professional meeting butlers will ensure that every event, whether it is a meeting, conference, dinner, or banquet, is tailored to suit your specific requirements and expectations.
